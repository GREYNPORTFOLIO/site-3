Creative Process

From initial sketch to finished artwork

1

Sketch

Definition of composition and basic shapes of the drawing with loose and gestural lines.

2

Lineart

Refinement of lines, definition of contours and details with precise strokes.

3

Base Colors

Application of base colors, palette definition and layer separation by elements.

4

Final Render

Application of shadows, lights, textures and special effects to finalize the artwork.

Techniques & Tools

Digital Process

  • Use of separate layers for each element
  • Digital painting method with custom brushes
  • Use of blending modes for special effects
  • Color correction and post-production adjustments

Tools

  • ibis Paint for illustration and painting
  • Adobe Illustrator for vectorization
  • Krita for digital painting

About the Artist

Learn more about me and my creative process

Biography

I am Greyn, a two-year-old Brazilian digital illustrator, acting professionally since early 2024. My artistic journey began in childhood with traditional design, but it was in digital that I found my true visual language. I have a passion for creating striking characters and expressive scenes, Mixing influences of anime, cyberpunk aesthetics and conceptual art. Vibrant, saturated and neon colors are the soul of my work, reflecting my search for visual impact and unique identity.

Style & Influences

My style combines vibrant colors, striking lighting and attention to detail to create immersive scenes. I'm influenced by artists like Krenz, Khyle, Nyantcha and Lalansane who influence my work, Although I'm still refining my current style, I'm always looking to step out of my comfort zone and explore new possibilities.

Terms of Service

  • Delivery time: 3-5+ days, depending on complexity
  • Up to 3 free revisions (varies by package)

Rights

  • Personal use only (commercial license available for +200% to +1000%)
  • I retain copyright of the artwork
  • You have right to use for personal purposes
  • I may refuse any request that goes against my principles

Do's & Don'ts

Don'ts

  • Extreme realistic style or 3D art
  • Animation or 3D modeling
  • NFTs
  • Caricatures (exaggerated or humorous style)
  • Commercial art without proper licensing
  • UI/UX layouts
  • Logo design
  • Political themes or ideological propaganda

Do's

  • NSFW
  • Gore
  • Original characters (OCs)
  • Fanarts
  • Furry
  • Mecha
  • Character concept design
  • Art for merchandising (shirts, stickers, etc.)
  • Animals and creatures
  • Chibis and emotes
  • Promotional artwork
